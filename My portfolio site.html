About Me

I'm Latifa Muzaffari, a dedicated professional with a background in computer science, digital marketing, and education. Originally from Daikundi, Afghanistan, I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science at the Asian University for Women in Bangladesh, graduating in May 2028. My diverse academic journey includes a coding boot camp at SheCodes, courses in Python and mobile app development at the American University of Afghanistan, a Digital Marketing and E-commerce Professional Certificate from Google Coursera, and Harvard's CS50 course on EDX. I've also explored liberal arts at the Xavier Institute of Management in India. Professionally, I’ve interned as a Frontend Developer at CodeAlpha in India, where I created various projects like a responsive music player and a smart calculator. I’ve also designed professional PowerPoint presentations at A-Z Consulting and taught English with the Jesuit Refugee Service Organization in Afghanistan. My technical skills include JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and Python, with experience in Git, Google Cloud Platform, and VS Code. Outside of work, I have a passion for poetry, literature, sketching, and journaling. I enjoy traveling and exploring new cultures, which enriches my perspective. I am excited about continuing to grow in computer science and education and look forward to future opportunities to learn and inspire. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
